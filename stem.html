STEM @ WORK & SCHOOL

Work:

I am the Jr. Test Engineer at Preteckt, a Memphis Start-Up company.

  • Preteckt is a startup company that uses machine learning and cloud based analysis to predict vehicle breakdowns
  • I began as an intern, where I learned skills such as linux, python, and hardware configurations.
  • I was then hired as an employee as a Jr. Test Engineer. Since then, I work with hardware and software.
    • Hardware: I build and configure their units with raspberry pis and prepare them for shipping
    • Software: I do QA Testing on their dashboard, work on the testing framework, and design the data science logs blog.
  • Preteckt has an extremely friendly, encouraging, and educational environment.
  • I am exposed to both the STEM and business aspects, and I look at the tech world with an entirely fresh set of eyes after throughout my time at Preteckt.

School:

I am involved in many STEM-related clubs and programs at school. My favorite organizations include the following:

  • Technology Student Association:
    • I learn how to be engineer through science and technology.
    • I participate in a variety of competitions within this organization. I have tested the structural integrity of a balsa wood bridge, built a life-size movie scene with recycled paper, designed a functional hovercraft, and more.
    • I am the Vice President of my school's chapter of TSA.
  • Science Olympiad:
    • I applied the skills I learned from class in chemistry, physics, and technology to participate in the rigorous Science Olympiad conventions, constructing sound structures and undergoing forensics investigations.
    • I built a tower from balsa wood modeled after the electrical towers. I created an game application using Scratch.
    • I am a Captain of the Science Olympiad team.
  • CyberPatriot:
    • We find and fix security vulnerabilities in a virtual environment on Windows and Linus OS in a competition environment
    • I am the Captain and President. I lead lessons to my peers, instructing them about the importance of cybersecurity in today's society and how they can personally secure information security on computers.
